A Mere Nightmare

The story centres on three characters: two friends on a quest to become successful entrepreneurs, and a girl, sister of a millionaire, and a successful businesswoman, who has one goal, to destroy her father. It is a tale of urban romance and the business world. Where two people meet by chance but are destined to be together. When the business world meets romance, their lives get entangled; In pursuit of success and love, each one facing a different life-changing decision, all on their own. Will this nightmare be over or is it just another peg for them?
