Mr. Gaurish Ninnekar

Gaurish Ninnekar, born in 1997 in a small city, Bidar, moved to Bengaluru and made himself at home in 2016 as an engineering student only to find his true passion in writing.

The known smart, intellectual Gaurish is also goofy, hilarious and a friend everyone should have. Inspired by his own and loved ones’ life experiences, his first Thriller-Romance Novel A mere nightmare allows him to open up and discover himself. Between being the best son, a great friend, a caring brother and a wonderful lover, he is now turning many nights' hard work into publishing an enthralling, top-notch gripping story making it a compulsive read to the modern-day young adults. Giving us this masterpiece, we're all thankful and love him eternally for being nothing less than Amazing in our lives.
